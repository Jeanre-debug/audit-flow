Features

Everything You Need to Run Your Auditing Business

Powerful tools designed for health & safety consultants and food safety officers. Manage clients, conduct audits, and deliver professional reports.

Digital Audits

Create custom audit templates, conduct inspections on any device, and generate instant compliance reports with scoring.

Issue Tracking

Automatically flag non-conformances, assign to team members, set priorities, and track resolution with photo evidence.

Multi-Site Management

Manage unlimited locations from a single dashboard. Track compliance across your entire organization effortlessly.

Real-Time Analytics

Visualize compliance trends, identify patterns, and make data-driven decisions with comprehensive dashboards and reports.

Daily Logging

PDF Reports

Generate professional audit reports with a single click. Share with stakeholders or download for regulatory submissions.
